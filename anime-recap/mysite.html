An Anime Recap Produced by CheeSe Dustyvoxx
Time period: 1st January, 2024 - 3rd November, 2024

What is this?

This is an anime recap highlighting some of the best anime I have watched in 2024. To be fair, only anime that I have watched for the first time will be count.

What behind my rating for each Anime?

Nothing

On my AniList page, you can clearly see the "REMEMBER THIS" quote. However, it's just a bunch of nonsense I wrote for fun.

The actual formula behind, is my feeling, my perspective, my very own tasting, it did complex as what I said. But emotionally and psychologically complex, there is no math here, just what I really thought.

My AniList

So, it's time for the show
There will be 5 Animes

Noragami ARAGATO

The sequel of Noragami

Noragami always brings me a joyful and marvelous storyline, not too complex, easy to understand. The simple storyline and austere way of telling, go along with the character development, it's hard to refuse Noragami as one of the best Anime I've watched.

DanMachi IV

The sequel of DanMachi III

DanMachi is both a funny and intense story. I love how Bell conquered his journey step-by-step and his effort was worth it. I still remember the scene when Wiene breaks her nails just to prove her humanity. It's really painful to watch. However, the bright side of Anime is also a nice thing to watch. Character development is psychologically amazing.
 

AniList DanMachi IV

Charlotte

Nao Tomori is cute

Speaking of Charlotte, I have to talk about the humor in it. There are various scenes that made me laugh, whether the scene is serious or not. I also love the simplicity inside the story of each character, simple but complex. Character development is also an appealing thing to watch. The whole show could be quite short and unfulfilled, but overall, a good Anime to watch.

AniList Charlotte

Sousou no Frieren

Frieren x Himmel best couple fr fr

Frieren brings me a joyful and comfy journey. Throughout the Anime, every pieces from the story makes the show itself slowly, but full of interestingness. Specially, I love how Himmel express his emotion to Frieren, really cute. Also with Fern and Stark, hopefully this couple will eventually become a couple.

Kuzu no Honkai

It's not a Morbid. This is psychological magic

It's hard to express my feelings about Kuzu no Honkai. It was like a roller coaster of emotion. I stick my eyes on the screen, frame by frame, not letting any moment drop out. The Anime digs deep into the story around the coming of age behavior with imaginative and artistic imagination by the author, making the story of Kuzu no Honkai so unique and fascinating. The only thing that made me really mad was the ending. It was an Opened Ending. NOT A FUCKING Closed Ending, REALLY MADE ME FUCKING RAGE. Anyway, I'm staying positive BUT STILL FUCKING MAD because when I was rushed through the "Decor", which is the sequel to the main storyline, it was just the ending for each character. For the very FUCKING FIRST TIME, I got hope for this. The ending for Hanabi and Mugi was actually quite close when they met up ONCE AGAIN and held their hands. IT'S STILL NOT CLOSED FUCKING ENDING, but you know what, maybe it's good enough.
